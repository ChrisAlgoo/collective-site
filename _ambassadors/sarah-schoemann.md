bio: Schoemann is the founder of Different Games conference and a founding member of Different Games Collective. She is also a Doctoral candidate in Digital Media at Georgia Tech where her research looks at the ways that DIY and accessible games inititiatives can support alternative community bulding amongst unnderrepresented members of the tech and games industry. She is a passionate advocate of games for personal expression and social critique and has been organizing community events in tech and arts spaces since 2007.
