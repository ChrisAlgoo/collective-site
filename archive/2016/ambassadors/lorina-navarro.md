bio: Lorina is a designer and ethnographer from the Philippines. Now a master's student in Human-Computer Interaction at Georgia Tech, she is researching how to co-create games with diverse users in order to design inclusive representations of their personal stories. At the moment, she is helping teach a class on Interactive Fiction, which uses Twine to write stories based on genre fiction conventions.
